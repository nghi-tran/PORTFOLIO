Dew Skincare Routine Application

Mobile skincare routine application that makes skincare accessible to beginners by simplify the process and resources.

Overview
UI/UX design
Figma
Fall 2021
Many skincare beginners face difficulties with how to start due to the complexity of this field. Dew is a mobile application designed to assist users to achieve their skin goals easier.
This app focuses on the diary-like tracking feature with a reminder system to make skincare simple and easy to follow.
Research
Targeted Groups
Research was conducted on a group of ten people ranging from skincare beginners to experts and in the age range of 19 to 40 years old.
Sample Questions
for the Interview
How long have you been using skincare?
How often do you use skincare?
Do you have a specific routine? If so, how complicated?
What are some skincare challenges that you have faced?
How do you keep up with your skincare routine?
Have you ever given up on your routine? If so, why?
Summary
80% use some sort of skincare daily
50% has a specific routine
70% has a simple routine
20% has a complicated routine
Main challenges recorded:
Overwhelmed by the amount of information and reviews from the internet
Not everyone has access to a dermatologist to consult
Lack of time and not able to remember every step or order to the routine
Difficulties in finding reliable source to buy products
Unable to see changes, many give up after a while
