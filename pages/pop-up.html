Richard Mille Pop Up Store

Pop-up shop to display and introduce
Richard Mille's new line of watch–RM 07-01.

Overview
Exhibition Design
Sketch Up
Fall 2021
This pop up shop is designed to fit inside a shipping container that can be moved and display at multiple locations. The style of the shop is based on Richard Mille's new line of watch–RM 07-01. This line is a completely out of the box style for Richard Mille since it's bright and colorful instead of the normal mature and ridgid style of the brand.
