Metamorphosis

Poster design for Visual Communication Design at Arizona State University Senior Show about social issues around the world.

Overview
Poster Design
Illustrator
Spring 2022
Every year, the senior class of the Visual Communication Design Program at Arizona State University host an exhibition at the end of the school year about social issues around the world that each student feels passionated about. This poster was designed to communicate the theme and meaning of the 30th senior exhibition.
